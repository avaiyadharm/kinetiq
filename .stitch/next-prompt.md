A high-fidelity interactive Pendulum Lab simulation page for the Kinetiq Physics Platform.
It should allow students to experiment with gravity, length, and mass in a tactile modernist environment.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
## Brand & Style
The design system is engineered for a high-performance physics simulation platform. The brand personality balances scientific rigor with the spark of discovery. Tactile Modernist style.
- **Precision Engineering:** Clean lines and a structured grid.
- **Interactive Feedback:** High-contrast states and physical metaphors.

## Colors
- **Vibrant Science Blue (#2563EB):** Primary structural navigation and "Standard" forces.
- **Laboratory Teal (#0D9488):** "Ready" indicators and secondary data categories.
- **Energy Orange (#F59E0B):** Active drag handles (the pendulum bob) and live warnings.
- **Background:** Deep Slate Charcoal (#1F2937).
- **Surface:** Deep Navy (#050f1c).

## Typography
- **Space Grotesk:** Headlines.
- **Inter:** Body text.
- **JetBrains Mono:** Numerical data/labels.

**Page Structure:**
1. Unified Kinetiq Header with Navigation.
2. Fluid Canvas Area (Center) showing a large, interactive pendulum.
3. Control Sidebar (Right, 320px) containing:
   - "Length" Slider (Laboratory Teal track, Energy Orange thumb).
   - "Mass" Slider.
   - "Gravity" Selector (Earth, Moon, Jupiter, Custom).
   - "Air Resistance" Toggle.
4. Data Bento Box (Bottom or floating) showing real-time Velocity, Period, and Energy (Potential/Kinetic) graphs.
5. "System Diagnostics" Log in the corner.
